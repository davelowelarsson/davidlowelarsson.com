## here's some of my work, enjoy!

This is my most recent showreel. This is also one of my first showreels, i must say it's fun digging up all your past work and seeing it all with new eyes.

I'm mostly proud of the explosion and the Specsavers glasses. The explosion was fun because i had never worked with fluids before and i learned a great deal about it, but i also worked a lot with particles during this project and it gave me some great new incites in that discipline.

The Specsavers project was a great learning experience, it was a project i worked on during my internship and i had the opportunity to work close to the client.
