## Whats a Centurion?

This project was during my internship at Bobby, the modeling was really fun and challenging during this project.

During this project I had a lot of responsibility, I was both grateful and surprised on the level of freedom i had during the project. I received directions but was allowed to do everything except the camera movements and this was because they reused camera movements from an earlier project.

I worked closely with another Artist during this project who gave me a lot of pointers and tips witch helped me learn a huge amount, he really opened my eyes to new tools and ways to tackle problems.

I also encountered a very strange bug during this project., a face normal was reversed but it couldn't be fixed by just reversing it to it's right position. I had to delete the face and then recreate it for the problem to go away.
